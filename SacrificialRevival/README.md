# Sacrificial Revival

Sacrificial Revival lets a living scout pay status costs to bring back a teammate instead of spawning a full inventory of items.

## Controls

- `F6`: revive the nearest dead or downed teammate.
- `F7`: pay the larger statue cost and drop a Scout Statue-like item near yourself if PEAK exposes one in `ItemDatabase`.

## Defaults

- Direct revive self cost: 30 injury, 10 curse.
- Revived teammate status: 20 hunger, 10 curse.
- Scout Statue self cost: 30 injury, 20 hunger, 10 curse.

All costs and keys are configurable in the BepInEx config file.
